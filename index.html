✅ Recommendations for Students

  • Beginners: Try BirdFont, FontStruct, Glyphr Studio, or Calligraphr
  • Adobe Users: FontSelf works well with Illustrator
  • Mac Users (Advanced): Consider Glyphs Mini or RoboFont
  • Windows Users (Advanced): FontCreator or FontLab
  • Open Source Fans: FontForge is free and powerful
